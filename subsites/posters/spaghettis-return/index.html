SPAGHETTI'S 2019 (I'm Home)
Noted NYC-based Italian-American supper club "Spaghetti's" will re-open Fall 2019 in Clinton Hill, Brooklyn. These dinners fuse elegant pan-european cuisine, avant garde performance, and powerfully romantic friendships.
Three events are planned for Sunday September 11, Sunday September 11, and Sunday September 11. Service begins at 8pm and lasts until the well runs dry. Your hungers will be satisfied.
Seating is limited and discretionary. Please contact Chef Kevin at kevin.e.roark@gmail.com with the subject line (Spaghetti's) to make a reservation. Ciao Bella
