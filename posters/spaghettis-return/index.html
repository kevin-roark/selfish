SPAGHETTI'S 2019 (I'm Home)
Noted NYC-based Italian-American supper club "Spaghetti's" will re-open Fall 2019 in Clinton Hill, Brooklyn. "Spaghetti's" dinners fuse elegant pan-european cuisine, avant garde performance, and powerful romantic friendship bonding opportunities.
Three events are planned for Sunday October 27, Sunday November 17, and Friday December 6. Service begins at 8:15pm. Every hunger will be satisfied. Event Concept & Lore revealed once guests are confirmed.
Seating is limited and discretionary. Please contact Chef Kevin at kevin.e.roark@gmail.com with the subject line (Spaghetti's) to make a reservation. Ciao Bella
